The Web 2.0 Era (2005-2010)

Web 2.0 was characterized by excessive use of gradients, glossy effects, reflections, rounded corners, and drop shadows. Every surface looked wet and reflective. Designers spent hours in Photoshop creating the perfect glossy button. The term "Beta" was slapped on everything (even Gmail was in beta for 5 years!). Sites used AJAX heavily, often showing loading spinners constantly. Everything needed to look like a shiny, polished consumer product.

Fun fact: Apple's influence was huge during this era. Everyone tried to make their websites look like iTunes or the iPhone OS.
